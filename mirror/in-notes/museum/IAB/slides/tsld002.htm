What is the IETF?

  • Internet Engineering Task Force

  • The sum of ~70 working groups

  • Meetings and email discussions open to all

  • More than 700 people at 3 meetings/year

  • Coordinated by Internet Engineering Steering Group

  • Architecture overview by Internet Architecture Board

  • IESG and IAB nominated from IETF

  • Legal umbrella from Internet Society (ISOC), which approves IAB membership
