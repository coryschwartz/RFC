Standards process (1)

  • Initial proposal from a scheduled birds-of-a-feather session (BOF)

  • Working Group (WG) charter and chair(s) approved by IESG and IAB (see RFC 1603)

  • WG produces & debates Internet Drafts by email and meetings

  • Chair must establish rough consensus - NO VOTING within the WG!

  • WGs are allowed to fail (their output is scrapped or tagged ‘historical’)

  • WG may produce ‘informational’ output
