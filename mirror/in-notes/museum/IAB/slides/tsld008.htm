What to do in the global context?

  • Strongly discourage regional or national standards efforts.

  • Strongly discourage bureaucracy. Open access to all standards groups and free access to all documents.

  • Do not create an elaborate academic dream

  • Standardise only finite, pragmatic, proven steps in the technology.

  • Use what exists (e.g. bit pipes from ITU-T standards, internetworking from Internet standards, encoding from JTC1...).
