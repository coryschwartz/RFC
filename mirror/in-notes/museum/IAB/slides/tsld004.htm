Standards process (2)

  • Debates leading to consensus are rough and tough and open to anybody

  • Complete running software is obligatory to advance on standards track

  • Emphasis is on technical clarity and simplicity

  • Standards go through [experimental], proposed, and draft standard status before approval as Internet Standards (see rfc 1602/1871)

  • All approvals are by formal IESG vote with appeals to IAB (see rfc1601)

  • No procedures are national or regional
