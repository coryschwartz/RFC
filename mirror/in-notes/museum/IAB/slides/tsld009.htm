What to do (continued)

  • Don’t bother to standardise the upper layers in advance. Just let the engineers loose.

  • Do not mandate technology that excludes developing countries. Especially, do not rely on ATM everywhere.

  • Liberalise international cryptography. Just say NO to mandatory key escrow/recovery.

  • Deregulate international bandwidth provision.

  • Seek the absolute minimum (ideally zero) in content regulation.
